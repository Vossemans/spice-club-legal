# Spice Club — Support

**Last updated:** 2026-05-11

Need help with the **Spice Club** mobile app? You're in the right place.

For anything not covered below, email **<davidjohansmolders@gmail.com>**.
We aim to reply within two business days.

## 1. Pairing with your partner

Spice Club is played by two paired accounts.

1. Open **Settings** in the app.
2. Find **Your pairing code** — a 6-character key.
3. Share that key with your partner (the in-app *Share* button on the
   onboarding screen sends it as a message).
4. Your partner opens the app, taps **I have a key →** on the welcome
   screen, and pastes your code.

Once the key is accepted, both accounts are linked and you can start a
game.

**The code didn't work.** Make sure your partner is typing the
6-character key exactly as it appears (it is case-insensitive). If you
recently unpaired and re-paired, your code may have changed — share the
current one from **Settings → Your pairing code**.

**I want to unpair.** Open **Settings → Unlink**. Your decks and history
stay on your own account; only the partner link is removed.

## 2. Subscription and free trial

Spice Club is a paid app with a 7-night free trial. One active
subscription on either account unlocks Spice Club for both partners.

- **Start a trial / subscribe** — tap **Try Spice Club for free** on the
  locked screen.
- **Cancel** — go to **Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions** on your
  iPhone, choose Spice Club, then **Cancel Subscription**. Cancellation
  takes effect at the end of the current period; you keep access until
  then.
- **Manage or change plan** — same place as cancel.
- **Restore a previous purchase** — in the app, open **Settings →
  Restore purchases**. Make sure you are signed in to the same Apple ID
  you used originally.

**I'm being charged but the app is locked.** Tap **Restore purchases**
in the app. If that doesn't help, email us with your Apple receipt
number and we'll investigate.

**My partner started a trial but I'm still locked.** Make sure you are
paired (see §1). One active subscription unlocks both partners, but the
pairing must be in place first.

## 3. Account, data, and deletion

- **Delete your account** — open **Settings → Delete account**. This
  permanently removes your account, profile, custom cards, games, and
  coupons. Your partner is automatically unlinked.
- **Reset the app on this device** — **Settings → Reset all data**
  wipes local state without deleting your account.
- **Export or access your data** — email us and we'll send you a copy.

See our [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY.html) for the full picture of what we
store and why.

## 4. Common questions

**Why can my partner see this card I never picked?**
You only ever see cards your partner picked *for you*. If a card
surprises you, it's because your partner selected it during their
setup. You can mark it **Don't show this card again** to hide it from
future games.

**Can we play on the same iPhone?**
Spice Club is designed for two separate devices. Each partner installs
the app on their own phone and pairs via the code.

**Can I change the heat level mid-game?**
The heat level set in onboarding shapes the cards you receive. To
adjust, open **Settings** and start a new game with a different heat
setting.

**Are my custom cards visible to anyone besides my partner?**
No. Custom cards are visible only to you and your paired partner.

**Notifications aren't arriving.**
Check that notifications are enabled for Spice Club in
**iOS Settings → Notifications → Spice Club**. If they are on and
nothing arrives, try toggling them off and on again, or reinstall the
app.
