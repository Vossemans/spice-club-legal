# Spice Club

**A private deck for two.**

Spice Club is a couples bingo game for iPhone. You and your partner
pair up, each pick the cards you'd like the other to play, and play
through the hand together over 7, 14 or 30 nights.

- **Mutual consent built in** — you only ever see cards your partner
  chose for you.
- **A heat dial** from *Faint* to *Supernova*, so the deck stays in
  your comfort zone.
- **Three curated packs** — Love, Spicy, Vacation — plus custom cards
  and coupons.
- **Private by design** — no social feed, no public profile,
  no advertising.

Spice Club is intended for adults aged 18 and older.
